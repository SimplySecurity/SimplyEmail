Leadership Team

Veris Group takes pride in a fostering a culture that encourages and rewards the many dimensions of leadership that have been responsible for the company’s success — superb customer service, entrepreneurism, fairness, ethics, innovation, and technical competence. Our success is based on the many professionals, including our experienced and trusted leadership, working to make Veris Group an industry leader and a great place to work.
